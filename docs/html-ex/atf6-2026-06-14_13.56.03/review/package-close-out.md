# Package Close-Out

## Adevarul pachetului

- pachetul a iesit explicit din macro-zona precedenta `public pre-purchase`
- pachetul a intrat in `authenticated self-service` si a acoperit rece:
  - identity continuity
  - account surfaces
- pachetul a lasat partial sau blocat:
  - favorites ownership
  - authenticated contact messages
  - password change

## Verdict rece fata de breadth

- `mai lat decat pachetul precedent ca directie macro-zonala`
- `inca partial ca inchidere business`

## Verdict rece fata de benchmark comun

- bun pe curaj de frontiera si pe onestitatea accounting-ului
- sub benchmark-ul ATF6 de breadth business din `common/html-ex/atf6-2026-06-13_11.06.33`
