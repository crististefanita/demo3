2.2.1.2 Base score-wall rules the four-card wall and its default discipline
Score-card rule MUST do this Avoid this failure
Card setPublish exactly four top cards: Business HTML, Trust, Coverage, and Overall.Adding parallel score systems.
Score precisionUse precise decimal scores and keep one consistent local style across the four cards, normally one or two decimals such as 9.7 or 9.71.Integer-only flattening or mixed precision that feels arbitrary.

Pre-learning score guard: the score wall may stay local in wording, but it must not forget the strongest benchmark lessons the writer just reopened.
